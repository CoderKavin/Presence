Inside the app

Built around four rules.

Posts need two people.
Every photo, every moment requires another verified person there. Bluetooth confirms it. Solo posts don't exist.
No feed. No follows.
You see what your circle made together. Nothing else. No scroll, no algorithm, no public profile.
In person, or not at all.
Friends are added by NFC tap or QR scan in real life. If you haven't met them, you can't add them.
A film at year-end.
Everything you and your circle made, automatically edited into a video at the end of the year. That's the artifact.

Questions
What if my school never hits 30?
We hold your number for six months. If your school hasn't unlocked by then, we delete it automatically. No stale lists, no lingering data.
When does it actually launch?
As soon as your school hits 30 sign-ups. Could be a week, could be a few months. We text you the day it does. That's the only message you'll get from us.
Is this for college or high school?
Both. Add whichever school you're at right now. If you're in your final year, sign up with the one you're at, not the one you're going to.
Do you sell my data?
No. No ads, no third parties, no resale. Your number is used to text you when your school unlocks. That's the only thing it does.
Principles

  • No location tracking
